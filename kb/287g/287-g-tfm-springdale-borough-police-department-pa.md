287(g) agreement between ICE and Springdale Borough Police Department.

Model: TFM
Signed: November 6, 2025
County: Allegheny County
State: PA
FIPS: 42003

## Controversy

Springdale's agreement was signed without public notice or council vote. Borough Manager Terry Carcella was unaware of the agreement when contacted by PublicSource: "That's news to me." He stated such an arrangement would normally be brought to council's attention.

Unlike neighboring Munhall and Stowe, Springdale officials **retained** the agreement after public scrutiny. At a December 17, 2025 public meeting, Borough Solicitor Craig Alexander cited state law permitting officer participation in federal task forces. A resident questioned whether the signing violated the Sunshine Act.

Backlash persisted for weeks after the December meeting. Springdale became one of the few Allegheny County municipalities to maintain its 287(g) agreement despite community opposition.
