287(g) agreement between ICE and Corydon Police Department.

Model: TFM
Signed: September 9, 2025
County: Harrison County
State: IN
FIPS: 18061

Corydon PD (population ~3,150) holds both TFM and WSO agreements — unusual for a small-town municipal police department. The Task Force Model grants proactive street-level enforcement authority, not just jail screening. Harrison County is adjacent to Clark County, whose Jeffersonville jail became a major ICE detention facility in April 2025. The county is on the Kentucky border, ~30 miles from Louisville, where the ICE sub-office made ~3,500 arrests between Jan 2025 and March 2026.

Note: The Harrison County Sheriff's Office (Sheriff Nick Smith) has NOT signed a 287(g) agreement. Only Corydon PD has this authority.
