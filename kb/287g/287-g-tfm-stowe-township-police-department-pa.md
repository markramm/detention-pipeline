## Reversal and Re-signing

Stowe Township is one of the most tangled 287(g) cases in Pennsylvania:

1. **Initial signing (late Nov 2025):** Deputy Chief Charles Wilker signed a 287(g) agreement without commission knowledge. Commission President Cheryl McDermott stated: "I did not hear anything about it."
2. **Reversal (Dec 4, 2025):** Agreement terminated after PublicSource inquiry and public pressure.
3. **Re-signing (Dec 11, 2025):** A new agreement was signed — again apparently without full commission awareness. ICE records show the December 11 date as the current active agreement.

The pattern of police chiefs signing 287(g) agreements unilaterally, without public votes or council approval, was flagged by the ACLU of Pennsylvania as a potential Sunshine Act violation.
