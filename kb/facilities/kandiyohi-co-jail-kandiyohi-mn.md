IGSA facility: Kandiyohi Co. Jail in Kandiyohi County, MN. Average daily population: 90.

Minnesota's largest ICE detention site by bed count. 190-bed operating capacity with ~150 beds designated for ICE. 22+ year IGSA — longest in Minnesota. Sheriff Tollefson signed 287(g) WSO May 2025, not yet operationalized.

Board publicly divided — chairman criticized ICE "cowboys." 100-person vigil Nov 2025.
