# Glades County Detention Center — Moore Haven FL

## Overview

The Glades County Detention Center is located at the Glades County Jail in Moore Haven, Florida. On **April 3, 2025**, ICE announced the reinstatement of an intergovernmental service agreement (IGSA) with the Glades County Board of County Commissioners, reestablishing federal immigration processing and creating up to **500 additional beds**.

## Key Details

- **IGSA reinstated**: April 3, 2025
- **Capacity**: Up to 500 beds
- **Operator**: Glades County
- **Contract type**: IGSA (intergovernmental service agreement)

## Deaths in Custody

A 19-year-old Mexican immigrant died in ICE custody at the Glades County Detention Center, drawing media attention to conditions at the facility.

## Context

Glades County is part of the broader Florida IGSA network that includes [[baker-county-fl-detention|Baker County]] and [[bradford-county-douglas-building|Bradford County]], through which the state provides county jail beds for ICE detention. These IGSA agreements — often facilitated by firms like [[sabot-consulting|Sabot Consulting]] — are a key mechanism for expanding detention capacity without federal facility construction.
