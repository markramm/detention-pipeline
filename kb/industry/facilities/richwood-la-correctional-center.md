# Richwood Correctional Center — Ouachita Parish

## Overview

Richwood Correctional Center in Monroe, Ouachita Parish, is a 1,129-bed medium-security facility operated by **LaSalle Corrections** for ICE. Along with Winn Correctional Center, Richwood has been operating **over its contractual capacity** by several dozen detainees.
