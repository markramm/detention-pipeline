# Pembina County Jail (Cavalier, ND)

## Overview

The **Pembina County Jail** in Cavalier is a small, 18-bed medium-security facility that serves dual functions as both a county jail and immigration detention facility. Located on the Canadian border, it has operated since 1994. Despite its tiny size, its IGSA with ICE and 287(g) agreement give it outsized significance in the border enforcement apparatus.

## Key Details

- **Location**: Cavalier, ND (FIPS 38067)
- **Capacity**: 18 beds
- **Opened**: 1994
- **Security level**: Medium
- **Function**: Immigration & County Detention Facility
- **Border proximity**: Directly on the North Dakota-Manitoba border

## Strategic Context

Pembina County sits on the Canadian border and is part of the Grand Forks Sector Border Patrol coverage area. The county has both an IGSA for detention and a 287(g) agreement, making it a dual-role enforcement node despite its small population.
