# Socorro TX Warehouse — ICE Processing Center

## Overview

DHS purchased a warehouse in Socorro, Texas for **$123 million** — a property previously valued at approximately **$11 million**. This represents the most extreme documented markup in the ICE warehouse acquisition program: approximately **1,000% overpayment**.

## Financial Details

- **Purchase price**: $123 million
- **Prior valuation**: $11 million
- **Markup**: ~1,000% (per Project Salt Box)
- The $112 million difference between purchase price and prior valuation represents a direct transfer from taxpayer funds to the property's prior owners/investors

## Context

Socorro is in the El Paso metro area, near the [[el-paso-tx-eastwind-mega-center|El Paso Eastwind Logistics Center]] mega-center. The extreme overpayment pattern documented by Project Salt Box shows that rushed, defense-channel procurement without normal price competition consistently produces purchases at multiples of market value.
