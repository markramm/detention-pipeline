# Allen Parish Public Safety Complex

## Overview

A 165-bed facility constructed in 2015, located just beyond Oberlin town limits. Unlike most Louisiana ICE facilities, this one is **sheriff-operated** rather than run by a private prison company. The Allen Parish Sheriff's Office contracts directly with DHS for ICE detainees.

Allen Parish scores **131 on the heatmap** — the **highest score of any Louisiana parish** — with 5 signal types (6 IGSAs, 13 287(g) agreements, ANC contract, 2 facilities, budget distress). The concentration of 6 IGSAs for a small parish is notable and suggests multiple overlapping agreements.
