# Pine Prairie ICE Processing Center

## Overview

GEO Group-operated facility in Pine Prairie, Evangeline Parish — a town of **1,490 people** where the ICE Processing Center holds **over 600 detainees** (nearly half the town's population). The facility has been operating at **1,010 detainees — more than double its contractual capacity**.

## Overcrowding Crisis

As of November 2025, conditions have severely deteriorated:
- Packed intake areas with **one shower, one toilet, one sink** for large numbers of incoming detainees
- Detainees sleeping on **bare mattresses on the floor** of holding cells
- **No access to law library or mail**
- Severely lacking medical care

Attorney Alexandra Smolyar documented the conditions in an August 2025 email: "Overcrowding is growing more dire by the day; medical care is severely lacking; there's no access to a law library or mail."

## Why It Matters

Pine Prairie is the quintessential example of rural Louisiana communities being transformed by detention infrastructure. The detainees outnumber the residents at a 1:2.5 ratio. The facility has a "long history of complaints alleging human rights abuses and civil rights violations" that predates the current overcrowding crisis.
