# Sarpy County Jail (Papillion)

## Overview

The **Sarpy County Department of Corrections** in Papillion, NE operates a county jail that houses ICE detainees under an Intergovernmental Service Agreement. The facility has a guaranteed minimum average daily population of 21 ICE detainees and receives $100 per day per detainee.

## Key Details

- **Location**: Papillion, NE (Omaha metro area)
- **Operator**: Sarpy County Department of Corrections
- **Contract type**: USMS IGA / ICE IGSA
- **Daily rate**: $100/day per detainee
- **Guaranteed minimum ADP**: 21 (as of Nov 28, 2025)

## Minnesota Transfer Role

As of early 2026, Sarpy County has housed immigrants arrested in Minnesota who were transferred to Nebraska. A Sarpy County communications manager confirmed the county's detention center was holding at least one Minnesota transfer.

## Notable Detainee Cases
