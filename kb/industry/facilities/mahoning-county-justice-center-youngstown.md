# Mahoning County Justice Center — County Jail ICE Detention

## Overview
The Mahoning County Justice Center in Youngstown became an ICE detention facility in late 2025, joining the adjacent CoreCivic-run Northeast Ohio Correctional Center. Sheriff Jerry Greene signed both an IGSA (bed space) and 287(g) agreement with ICE. Together with the CoreCivic facility, Youngstown houses approximately 500 ICE detainees.

## Key Details
- **ICE rate**: $125 per inmate per day
- **Annual revenue**: Estimated $4.5 million/year from ICE contract
- **Average daily population**: ~100 ICE detainees (as of September 2025: 108 ICE inmates, 142 USMS inmates, 2 BOP)
- **287(g) agreement**: Sheriff Greene signed a 287(g) agreement, one of the first three in Ohio (alongside Butler and Seneca counties)
- **Community opposition**: ~100 protesters held a peaceful demonstration at the Mahoning County Sheriff's Office in April 2025
- **Airport**: Youngstown-Warren Regional Airport is 7th busiest for ICE deportation flights nationally (202 flights, Feb-Jul 2025)
