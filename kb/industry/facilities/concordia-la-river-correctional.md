# River Correctional Center — Concordia Parish

## Overview

The River Correctional Center is located in Ferriday, Concordia Parish, owned and operated by **LaSalle Corrections** under an IGSA with the Concordia Parish Sheriff's Office (signed 2019). Capacity of approximately 600 adult male detainees. As of May 2019, held 510 detained immigrants, primarily nationals of Honduras, Guatemala, and El Salvador.
