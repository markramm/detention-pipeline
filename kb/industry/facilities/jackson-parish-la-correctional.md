# Jackson Parish Correctional Center

## Overview

Jackson Parish Correctional Center in Jonesboro is a 1,252-bed medium-security facility managed by **LaSalle Corrections** for ICE. The facility's financial structure illustrates the detention economy: ICE pays the Jackson Parish Sheriff's department **$74/day per detainee**, approximately **three times what the state pays** to house someone convicted of a crime. This per-diem includes translator and healthcare costs but represents a significant windfall for a budget-distressed parish.
