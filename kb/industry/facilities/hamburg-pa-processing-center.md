# Hamburg PA ICE Processing Center

One of six confirmed processing center purchases in ICE's Detention Reengineering Initiative. Planned capacity 1,000-1,500 beds with average stays of 3-7 days. Part of the Pennsylvania cluster (along with [[upper-bern-pa-warehouse]] and [[tremont-pa-mega-center]]). Hamburg is the post-office town for the Upper Bern Township site at 3501 Mountain Road, so this entry and [[upper-bern-pa-warehouse]] likely describe the same Berks County processing center.

As of June 2026 the site is **blocked** by PA DEP orders and under appeal at the Environmental Hearing Board (DEP/DHS settlement discussion ordered by June 8, 2026). See [[pa-berks-upper-bern-ice-warehouse]].
