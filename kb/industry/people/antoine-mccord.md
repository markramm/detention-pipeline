# Antoine McCord — DHS CIO

## The Conflict

Antoine McCord, **Chief Information Officer of the Department of Homeland Security**, is a former **Anduril Industries employee** who holds active Anduril equity (RSUs, 401(k), ESPP, stock) and has Anduril employment agreements.

As DHS CIO, McCord oversees technology procurement for **ICE, CBP, and all DHS components** — the agencies that award contracts to Anduril's competitors and potentially to Anduril itself.

## The Thiel Network CIO Pipeline

McCord's appointment creates a pattern: the **two officials responsible for federal technology procurement** both come from Peter Thiel's defense portfolio:

| Official | Role | Former Employer |
|----------|------|----------------|
| **Antoine McCord** | DHS CIO | **Anduril** (Thiel-backed) |
| **Gregory Barbaccia** | Federal CIO, OMB | **Palantir** (Thiel co-founded) |

The DHS CIO who buys surveillance tech for ICE is from Anduril. The OMB Federal CIO who oversees all federal IT procurement is from Palantir. Both hold equity in their former employers.
