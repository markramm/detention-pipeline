# Mississippi County Meeting Minutes Sources

Community-contributed primary source URLs for monitoring county government activity related to ICE detention in Mississippi. Submitted via GitHub tips by Brandy Armstrong (April 15, 2026).

## Adams County (FIPS 28001)

Home of the Adams County Correctional Center — the 2nd largest ICE detention facility in the country (2,260 beds, CoreCivic).

- **Board of Supervisors meetings**: [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1HtmGtH_xoeuC7ysLFjT7wAsnDS40UYF)
- Format: Video recordings
- Monitoring priority: High — CoreCivic contract renewals, conditions oversight, community response

## Harrison County (FIPS 28047)

Gulf Coast county with the Hancock County Public Safety Complex nearby — transit hub for ICE detainees.

- **Board of Supervisors**: [Agenda & minutes portal](https://harrisoncountyms.gov/goverment/board_of_supervisors/board_agendas___minutes/index.php)
- Format: PDF documents (post-2009 meetings use agenda management system with hyperlinks)
- Note: Large file sizes for some meetings

## Hancock County (FIPS 28045)

Home of the Hancock County Public Safety Complex — Gulf Coast ICE transit facility.

- **Board of Supervisors + Planning & Zoning**: [CivicClerk portal](https://hancockcoms.portal.civicclerk.com/)
- Format: Minutes, videos, and agendas via CivicClerk CMS
- Monitoring priority: High — facility expansion, zoning decisions

## How to Use

These sources enable:
1. Monitoring for new ICE-related agenda items (contract votes, facility proposals, 287(g) discussions)
2. Cross-referencing commission decisions with our existing Mississippi coverage
3. Building a timeline of how county governments engaged with ICE proposals
