# MVM Inc. — ICE Transportation Contract

[[mvm-inc|MVM, Inc.]] holds one of the largest cumulative ICE contract portfolios for detainee transportation, with **$1.1 billion in total obligated contracts** and **$145.1 million in new 2025 contracts** alone.

## Scope of Services

MVM's transportation contract covers two distinct populations:

### Adult Detainee Transportation
- Ground and air transport of ICE detainees between facilities
- Escort services during transfers
- Support for ERO enforcement operations

### Unaccompanied Children Transportation
- Transport of unaccompanied migrant children from CBP custody to HHS/ORR shelters
- Commercial flight escorts for minors
- Family unit transfers
- Original UAC contract (2014): $162 million, later expanded to $128 million in additional obligations

## Political Connections

The Marquez family, which owns MVM, has been identified by OpenSecrets as major Trump donors who are "reaping billions in ICE contracts." Kevin Marquez purchased the company from his father Dario Marquez Jr. in 2015.
