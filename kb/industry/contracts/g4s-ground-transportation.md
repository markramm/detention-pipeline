# G4S Secure Solutions — ICE Ground Transportation

[[g4s-secure-solutions|G4S Secure Solutions]] (Allied Universal subsidiary) provides armed ground transportation and detention officer services across multiple ICE field offices under a series of task orders and delivery contracts.

## Known Contracts

| Field Office | Value | Status |
|-------------|-------|--------|
| San Antonio | TBD | Active |
| Los Angeles | TBD | Extended Sep 2025 (3 months) |
| Phoenix | TBD | Active |
| San Francisco | TBD | Extended Sep 2025 (3 months) |
| Salt Lake City | $10.4M | Awarded Mar 2026 |
| San Diego | Up to $100M | Solicited Dec 2025; award Q2 FY2026 |

## Services Under Contract

- Armed transportation of detainees in government-owned and contractor-owned vehicles
- Detention officer staffing for custody during transport
- Courtroom guarding
- 24-hour, year-round operations with trained, uniformed detention officers

## Contract Structure

The San Diego solicitation reveals the structure: firm-fixed-price, indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) multiple-award contracts, set aside for small businesses, running through January 14, 2031.
