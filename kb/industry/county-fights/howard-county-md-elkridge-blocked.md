# Howard County MD — Stealth ICE Facility Blocked

## The Fight

A privately owned ICE detention facility at 6522 Meadowridge Road in Elkridge was being quietly developed by **Genesis GSA Strategic One LLC** since at least 2022. The building permit was obtained in August 2025 by McKeever Services (Fairfax, VA), with scope describing a "detention facility, detainee processing and secured waiting area." The project went largely unnoticed until early 2026.

## Community Response

- **February 2, 2026**: **Hundreds rallied** against the facility in Elkridge
- Same day: County Executive **Calvin Ball revoked the building permit**
- Ball submitted **emergency legislation** to ban permitting of privately owned buildings operating as detention centers
- Howard County Council passed the ban

## Key Actors

- **County Executive Calvin Ball** — revoked permit, introduced emergency legislation
- **Genesis GSA Strategic One LLC** — property owner
- **McKeever Services** — permit applicant (Fairfax, VA-based)
- **Community organizers** — rapid mobilization after discovery

## Why It Matters

This fight demonstrates the **stealth pathway** for ICE detention expansion — private contractors quietly obtaining building permits without public notice. Howard County's rapid response (discovery to ban in days) shows that community vigilance and responsive local government can block these projects. The model is replicable: monitor building permits for detention-related language.
