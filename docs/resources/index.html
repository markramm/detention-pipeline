Resources

Organizations, tools, and data sources for detention accountability — from legal hotlines for detained people to FOIA templates to flight trackers.

Monitoring & Data

Detention Watch Network ↗ national

National coalition working to end immigration detention. Publishes reports on conditions and expansion. Coordinates the #CommunitiesNotCages campaign.

Freedom for Immigrants ↗ national

National hotline for detained people, detention facility map, abuse and death documentation, and community bond fund network directory.

Global Detention Project ↗ international

Geneva-based research center mapping immigration detention worldwide. Maintains detailed US facility profiles.

National Immigrant Justice Center ↗ national

Tracks detention conditions, publishes transparency reports, documents solitary confinement and medical neglect in ICE facilities.

TRAC Immigration (Syracuse University) ↗ national

Uses FOIA to obtain and analyze granular ICE/CBP data on detentions, deportations, court backlogs, and enforcement trends. Essential for data journalists.

WITNESS Media Lab / Eyes on ICE ↗ national

Tools and methodologies for safely, ethically, and effectively documenting ICE operations and detention conditions through video and media.

Legal Aid & Know-Your-Rights

ACLU Immigrants' Rights Project ↗ national

Litigates major constitutional challenges to detention policies. Published 2024 report documenting 63 ICE detainee deaths (2017-2023).

Asian Americans Advancing Justice — Atlanta ↗ georgia

Runs the 'Shut Down Folkston' campaign. Litigation Director Meredyth Yoon leads legal challenges to Folkston IPC conditions.

Florence Immigrant & Refugees Rights Project ↗ arizona

Free legal services to detained immigrants in Arizona. One of the largest legal aid providers in the detention system. Filed 53 complaints about Folkston IPC.

Immigrant Defense Project ↗ new-york

New York-based. Operates statewide ICE raid hotline and rapid response network. Publishes ICE raid tracking data.

Innovation Law Lab ↗ national

Builds technology and legal infrastructure to support immigrant rights. Filed formal complaints about Folkston IPC.

RAICES ↗ texas

Refugee and Immigrant Center for Education and Legal Services. Major bond fund and legal services provider in Texas.

Southern Poverty Law Center — Immigrant Justice ↗ southeast

Litigates on behalf of detained immigrants in the Southeast. Investigates detention conditions. Co-signed Folkston complaint.

Resistance & Community Organizing

Make the Road New York ↗ northeast

Grassroots community organizing across NY, NJ, CT, PA, NV. Combines legal services, community resistance, and political advocacy.

Mijente ↗ national

Latinx/Chicanx political organizing hub. Runs #FreeThemAll and NotOneMore campaigns. Coordinates direct action against ICE operations and detention expansion.

United We Dream ↗ national

Largest immigrant youth-led network. Operates rapid response infrastructure including ICE raid alert systems and know-your-rights trainings.

Investigative Journalism

Austin Kocher ↗ national

Academic researcher and geographer tracking detention expansion geography. Affiliated with TRAC Syracuse.

Bolts Magazine ↗ national

Covers the intersection of local elections, prosecutors, and criminal/immigration policy. Useful for understanding how local officials enable or resist detention.

Borderland Talk (Jenn Budd) ↗ national

Former senior Border Patrol agent turned whistleblower. Tracks facility design, due process obstruction, and the IGSA-to-sheriff model shift.

Documented ↗ new-york

New York-focused immigration newsroom covering enforcement, detention, and immigrant community impact.

The Intercept — Immigration ↗ national

Investigative outlet with significant detention and deportation reporting including leaked documents and enforcement policy exposes.

The Marshall Project ↗ national

Nonprofit newsroom covering criminal justice. Sustained detention reporting including deaths in custody, conditions, and private prison profiteering.

Policy & Research

American Immigration Council ↗ national

Publishes factsheets, legal analyses, and policy briefs on detention and enforcement. Research reports on facility-by-facility tracking.

Brennan Center for Justice ↗ national

Covers executive power overreach, due process, and the legal framework enabling detention expansion. Published 'How ICE's Budget Boom Is Changing Immigration Detention.'

FWD.us ↗ national

Published 'Force Multiplier' report documenting the 287(g) arrest bounty system — $1.4-2B to local law enforcement with performance bonuses based on arrest counts.

Human Rights First ↗ national

Documents asylum and detention policy impacts. Publishes ICE Flight Monitor monthly reports tracking deportation and domestic transfer flights.

Prison Policy Initiative ↗ national

Source for 287(g) agreement data used in this site. Published 'How is your local government collaborating with ICE?' research guide.

Vera Institute of Justice ↗ national

Source for IGSA facility data used in this site. Pioneered the SAFE Network providing legal counsel to detained immigrants.

FOIA & Public Records

DocumentCloud ↗ national

Platform for uploading, annotating, and sharing source documents. Widely used by journalists to publish FOIA results and detention-related documents.

MuckRock ↗ national

Platform for filing, tracking, and sharing public records requests. Large archive of ICE/CBP FOIA results. Active FOIA request for ICE response to Folkston OIG report.

Reporters Committee for Freedom of the Press ↗ national

State-by-state open records law guides. Essential for filing state and county records requests related to IGSA agreements and detention contracts.

Facility & Flight Tracking

ADS-B Exchange ↗ national

Open-source flight tracking. Does not filter government aircraft (unlike FlightAware). Used by researchers to monitor ICE Air Operations flights.

Courier Newsroom — ICE Warehouse Map ↗ national

Most comprehensive publicly available map of ICE warehouse locations. Google Map with clickable locations showing status and community responses.

DemLabs / Project Salt Box — ICE Facility Tracker ↗ national

Google Map tracking proposed and confirmed detention sites with property-level detail including address, ownership, and sale status.

Data Tools

CourtListener / RECAP ↗ national

Free archive of PACER documents. Reduces cost of accessing federal court records — lawsuits against facilities, habeas petitions, conditions litigation.

OpenCorporates ↗ international

Search corporate registrations to trace shell companies and subsidiaries involved in detention facility ownership and warehouse purchases.

SAM.gov ↗ national

Federal contract opportunities and entity registrations. Track new detention-related solicitations and contract awards.

SEC EDGAR ↗ national

For researching GEO Group and CoreCivic. 10-K filings reveal facility locations, contract details, revenue by segment, and expansion plans.

USAspending.gov ↗ national

Federal spending database. Search ICE contracts by NAICS code, recipient, and location. Primary data source for the ANC contract signals tracked on this site.
