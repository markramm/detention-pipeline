Florence Service Processing Center — Pinal County AZ (ICE / Akima Global Services)

Pinal · Arizona · Operator: ICE (guard/food/transport by Akima Global Services) · unknown beds

Florence Service Processing Center — ANC Sole-Source Pipeline

Overview

The Florence Service Processing Center (SPC) is an ICE-operated processing facility in Florence, Arizona. Guard, food, and transportation services are contracted to Akima Global Services, LLC — an Alaska Native Corporation subsidiary that receives sole-source federal contracts without competitive bidding under the SBA 8(a) program.

ANC Contract Pipeline

The Akima contracts are escalating year over year:
