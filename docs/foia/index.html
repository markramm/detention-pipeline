Public Records Request Generator

Enter your county and state to generate a ready-to-send public records request. This letter requests communications between your county and detention consultants, closed-session agendas, and any intergovernmental agreements related to immigration detention.

Your Public Records Request

How to send this

  • Find your county clerk or records custodian's email on the county website
  • Send this letter as the body of an email with subject: "Public Records Request — [Your County]"
  • Keep a copy of the email for your records
  • If you don't get a response within 10 business days, follow up
  • When you get records, submit what you find as a tip

What this request covers

  • Consultant communications — emails, letters, proposals, and presentations from Sabot Consulting, CoreCivic, GEO Group, Management & Training Corporation, Immigration Centers of America, or any entity regarding immigration detention
  • Closed-session agendas — executive session or closed meeting agendas and minutes mentioning detention, ICE, IGSA, bed capacity, or federal partnerships
  • Intergovernmental agreements — any IGSAs, MOUs, or cooperative agreements with ICE, DHS, or the U.S. Marshals Service
  • Financial analyses — revenue projections, cost-benefit analyses, or fiscal impact studies related to detention facility proposals
  • Sheriff communications — correspondence between the sheriff's office and ICE, detention consultants, or the National Sheriffs' Association regarding detention partnerships
