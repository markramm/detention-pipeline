## CRITICAL CONFLICTS (Procurement-Adjacent Positions)

### John Phelan — Secretary of the Navy ($791M total assets)
- **Multiple Blue Owl holdings**: Blue Owl Capital (OWL), Blue Owl Healthcare Opportunities III LP
- **WHY THIS MATTERS**: The Navy Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) administers WEXMAC-TITUS — the military contracting vehicle through which ALL ICE warehouse purchases are routed. The Secretary of the Navy oversees NAVSUP. Phelan holds Blue Owl investments while his department administers the contract vehicle purchasing Blue Owl's warehouses.
- Also holds 26North BDC (his own firm) and extensive alternative investment portfolio

### John Russell McGranahan — General Counsel, GSA

## THE STRUCTURAL CONFLICT

**Four DOD officials** hold Blue Owl investments. DOD administers WEXMAC-TITUS. The **Secretary of the Navy** — whose command (NAVSUP) literally runs the contract vehicle — holds multiple Blue Owl positions including a healthcare opportunities fund. The **Assistant Secretary of the Navy for Installations** — who oversees the physical infrastructure side — also holds Blue Owl stock.

The chain: Blue Owl owns warehouses → DHS buys them through WEXMAC-TITUS (administered by Navy/NAVSUP) → The Secretary of the Navy holds Blue Owl investments → The purchases generate returns for Blue Owl investors including the Secretary of the Navy.

This is not a passive index fund exposure. Several of these officials hold **direct fund investments** (Popolo: Blue Owl Capital Diversified Holdings LLC; Leon: Blue Owl Real Estate Fund VI; Gould: Blue Owl RE NLP Fund P LP; Phelan: Blue Owl Healthcare Opportunities III LP) — these are illiquid alternative investments that require active subscription, not incidental stock holdings.
