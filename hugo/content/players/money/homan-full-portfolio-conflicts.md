# Thomas D. Homan — Border Czar Conflict Portfolio

Source: ProPublica Trump Team Financial Disclosures. Total reported assets: $1.2M.

## The Pattern

Homan holds financial ties to companies spanning **every major ICE contractor category**: surveillance data, detention operations, commercial real estate brokerage, industrial warehousing, and private equity with detention exposure.

| Holding | Category | Connection |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **GEO Care** consulting fees | Detention operations | GEO Group subsidiary; Homan received direct compensation |
| **RELX PLC** (LexisNexis parent) | Data brokering | ICE uses LexisNexis LEIDS for targeting ($22.1M contract) |
| **CBRE Group Inc.** stock | Real estate brokerage | CBRE brokered the Williamsport MD sale to DHS |
| **Prologis Inc.** stock | Industrial REIT | World's largest warehouse owner; potential future ICE targets |
| **Blackstone Inc.** stock | Private equity / CRE | Massive industrial real estate holdings |

Homan also received compensation from: DCI Group, Fox News Corps, Heritage Foundation, Homeland Strategic Consulting LLC, America First Policy Institute.

## Why This Matters

Homan is the **Border Czar** — the official who directs ICE enforcement operations. His policy decisions directly determine:
- How many people are arrested (generating demand for detention beds → GEO Group revenue)
- Which data systems ICE uses for targeting (→ LexisNexis/RELX revenue)
- How many facilities ICE needs (→ warehouse demand → CBRE/Prologis)

The Raskin/Warren letter names Homan as having "reportedly helped contractors secure contracts for personal profit." The Lewandowski pay-to-play investigation includes allegations that Homan and Lewandowski helped contractors secure contracts.
