In September 2025, ICE's Homeland Security Investigations (HSI) finalized a **$9.2 million** sole-source contract with [Clearview AI](/entry/clearview-ai/) for biometric facial matching software.

## Contract Structure

- **Base period:** One year
- **Options:** Three one-year extensions
- **Total potential value:** $9.2 million

## Stated Purpose

The contract provides facial recognition for two investigative missions:
1. Child sexual exploitation cases
2. Investigations into assaults on law enforcement officers

However, once deployed, the technology's actual use is difficult to oversee or constrain to stated purposes.

## Technology

Clearview provides access to a proprietary database of **50+ billion facial images** scraped from the internet -- described in procurement documents as "the largest and most comprehensive facial recognition database available."

## Sole-Source Justification

ICE prepared the sole-source justification in August 2025, citing Clearview's unique database and technology as grounds for bypassing competitive bidding.
