## Overview

Bradley County (Cleveland, TN) has adopted **two 287(g) models** — the Jail Enforcement Model (JEM) and the Warrant Service Officer (WSO) model — giving it unusually broad immigration enforcement authority for a county its size. It carries the highest 287(g) signal score (7) in the heatmap data despite having only that one signal type.

## Key Details

### 287(g) agreements
- **Jail Enforcement Model (JEM)**: Deputies trained to identify immigration violations of people arrested on criminal charges
- **Warrant Service Officer (WSO)**: Deputies can serve ICE warrants on jailed individuals
- Sheriff Steve Lawson updated the Bradley County Commission's Law Enforcement Committee on the program

### Context
- Selected BCSO deputies received ICE training under the program
- Deputies work with ICE to identify undocumented immigrants arrested and charged with crimes in Bradley County
- Part of East Tennessee's expanding network of 287(g) agencies
