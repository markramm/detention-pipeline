government/corrections-dependent economy; low employment ratio; housing stress; low post-secondary education

Full flags: Government, Housing Stress, Low Employment, Low PostSecondary Ed

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.
