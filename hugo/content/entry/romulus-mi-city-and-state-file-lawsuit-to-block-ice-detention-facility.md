City of Romulus and State of Michigan filed suit March 24, 2026 to block ICE's conversion of 7525 Cogswell St warehouse to detention.

Timeline:
- Feb 2026: ICE purchases warehouse without notice
- Feb 17: ICE confirms purchase
- Feb 27: AG Nessel demands halt
- Late Feb: 700-800 protesters at City Hall
- Mar 12: Axios reports Governor Whitmer silent while AG fights
- Mar 24: City and State file lawsuit

Legal arguments: near schools, residential area, floodplain, inadequate infrastructure.

Political dynamics: Governor Whitmer silent, AG Nessel leading opposition. Pattern seen in other states.

Source: Michigan Public, Michigan AG Office, Axios, CNN
