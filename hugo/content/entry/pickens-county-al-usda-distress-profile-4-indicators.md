USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags Pickens County, AL with 4 distress indicators:

government/corrections-dependent economy; losing population; persistent poverty (20%+ poverty rate for 30+ years); low employment ratio

Full flags: Government, Low Employment, Population Loss, Persistent Poverty

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.

Nonmetro: metro

Source: USDA ERS County Typology Codes, 2025 edition
