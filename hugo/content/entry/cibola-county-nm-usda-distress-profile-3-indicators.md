USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags Cibola County, NM with 3 distress indicators:

government/corrections-dependent economy; persistent poverty (20%+ poverty rate for 30+ years); low employment ratio

Full flags: Government, Low Employment, Persistent Poverty

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.
