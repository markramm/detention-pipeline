USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags Hidalgo County, NM with 3 distress indicators:

government/corrections-dependent economy; losing population; persistent poverty (20%+ poverty rate for 30+ years)

Full flags: Government, Population Loss, Persistent Poverty

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.

Nonmetro: nonmetro
