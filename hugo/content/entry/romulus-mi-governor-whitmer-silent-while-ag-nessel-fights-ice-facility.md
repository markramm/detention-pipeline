Governor Whitmer has been notably silent on the Romulus ICE warehouse while AG Dana Nessel has led the state-level opposition. Axios covered the political split (Mar 12, 2026).

This mirrors a pattern seen in other states where governors avoid confrontation with the federal government while AGs take the lead on detention fights.

The silence is itself a communications signal — it suggests political calculation about the cost of opposing ICE publicly at the governor level.
