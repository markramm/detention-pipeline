Bradford County commission approved an IGSA for ICE detention in a 3-2 vote. The Bradford County sheriff cited a '$5.25 to $5.5 million dollar shortfall' as justification.

The 'Douglas Building' — county-owned property — was identified as the facility. Sabot's model: help the county monetize what it already owns.

Bradford County is one of two Florida examples Sabot uses in its pitch deck ('Look at Baker County. Look at Glades County. Run by sheriffs being fully reimbursed.').
