# Palantir ICM Contract (2022)

ICE's Investigative Case Management (ICM) system is built and operated by [[palantir-technologies|Palantir Technologies]]. The current contract was awarded in 2022, valued at **$139.3 million**, and set to expire in April 2026.

## System Capabilities

ICM is the official record system for ICE investigations. It:
- Logs and manages criminal and civil investigation files
- Structures case workflows
- Documents investigative activity
- Integrates with ICE Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO) deportation records

## Sole-Source Renewal

ICE has indicated the contract is slated for **sole-source renewal** because the agency cannot find another company that makes a comparable system. This pattern -- building critical infrastructure on a proprietary platform, then citing sole-source justification for renewals -- effectively locks ICE into permanent dependency on Palantir.
