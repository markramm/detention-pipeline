USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags McKinley County, NM with 4 distress indicators:

government/corrections-dependent economy; persistent poverty (20%+ poverty rate for 30+ years); low employment ratio; housing stress

Full flags: Government, Housing Stress, Low Employment, Persistent Poverty

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.

Nonmetro: nonmetro

Source: USDA ERS County Typology Codes, 2025 edition
