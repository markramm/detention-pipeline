New Mexico advanced HB 9 (Immigrant Safety Act) which would ban public bodies from entering ICE detention agreements (IGSAs). This is preemptive legislation responding to the detention expansion pipeline.

States introducing such legislation are states where the pitch is active. NM has 18 IGSA facilities — third highest after TX and CA.

Conversely, states WITHOUT such protections are the open field for Sabot and similar consultants.

Source: RAMM research, NM legislature
