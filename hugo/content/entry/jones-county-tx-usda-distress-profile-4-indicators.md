USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags Jones County, TX with 4 distress indicators:

government/corrections-dependent economy; losing population; low employment ratio; low post-secondary education

Full flags: Government, Low Employment, Low PostSecondary Ed, Population Loss

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.

Nonmetro: metro

Source: USDA ERS County Typology Codes, 2025 edition
