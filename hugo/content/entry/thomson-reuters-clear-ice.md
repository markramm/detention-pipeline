## Primary Contract — $22.8M

[Thomson Reuters Special Services](/players/contractors/thomson-reuters/) holds a five-year, **$22.8 million** contract with DHS for ICE's use of a "law enforcement investigative database subscription." The 2025 option year explicitly notes "provision of license plate reader data," confirming CLEAR provides ICE with ALPR data in addition to personal records.

## West Publishing Contract — $1.7M + $1.9M

West Publishing, a Thomson Reuters subsidiary, entered a **$1.7 million** contract with DHS for ICE CLEAR access in April 2023. A **$1.9 million** option was exercised in March 2025.

## Data Provided

Through these contracts, ICE accesses:
- Names, addresses, Social Security numbers
- Utility records (used to confirm current residence)
- Phone records
- Vehicle registration and license plate data
- Social media posts
- Real-time geolocation data
- Arrest and court records
- Health-care provider information
