Curry County signed the **only 287(g) agreement in New Mexico** in May 2025. Then the legislature passed the **Immigrant Safety Act (HB 9)**, signed by Governor Lujan Grisham, banning local ICE detention contracts, 287(g) agreements, and use of public land for immigration detention. Effective May 20, 2026 — Curry County must terminate.

The sheriff publicly decried the law. The legislature considered a **$10.5M compensation bill** for counties losing ICE detention revenue (Torrance, Cibola, Otero) — revealing how dependent rural NM counties have become on detention.
