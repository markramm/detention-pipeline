USDA Economic Research Service county typology flags Frio County, TX with 4 distress indicators:

energy-transition county (mining-dependent); persistent poverty (20%+ poverty rate for 30+ years); low employment ratio; low post-secondary education

Full flags: Mining, Low Employment, Low PostSecondary Ed, Persistent Poverty

This county already has an IGSA detention facility. The combination of existing federal detention relationship + economic distress = high vulnerability to expansion pitch.

Nonmetro: nonmetro

Source: USDA ERS County Typology Codes, 2025 edition
