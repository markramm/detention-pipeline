ICE purchased this commercial warehouse in February 2026 without notice to state, city, or public. Planned as a Regional Processing Center for ~500 migrants. ICE claims 1,458 jobs and $33M tax revenue.

Located within a mile of an elementary school and middle school, abuts residential neighborhoods, lies within a floodplain that flooded in 2025.

Michigan AG Nessel and City of Romulus filed suit March 24, 2026 to block conversion. 700-800 protesters at City Hall.

This is the federal purchase model — bypasses county commission votes entirely, unlike the Sabot/IGSA model.
