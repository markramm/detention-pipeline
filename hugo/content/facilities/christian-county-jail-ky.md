## Overview

The Christian County Jail in Hopkinsville, KY held an average of 104 ICE detainees as of February 2026. The facility is notably opaque: none of the detainees are explicitly identified as immigration-related on the jail's website, creating a transparency gap between local, state, and federal records.

## Key Facts

- **ICE detainees (Feb 2026)**: 104 average
- **Total population**: 754
- **Rated capacity**: 611 beds
- **Overcrowding**: 23% over capacity (143 people above)
- **Transparency**: No immigration detainees identified on jail website despite ICE reporting 104

## Why It Matters

Christian County exemplifies the transparency problem: if ICE reports 104 detainees but the local jail website shows zero, families and advocates cannot locate people in immigration custody. This creates conditions for people to "disappear" into the detention system.
