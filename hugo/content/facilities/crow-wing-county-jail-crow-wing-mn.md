IGSA facility: Crow Wing County Jail in Crow Wing County, MN. Average daily population: 20 (historical); 30-50 ICE detainees as of Jan 2026.

Sheriff Eric Klang signed dual 287(g) agreements (TFM March 25, 2025; WSO March 26, 2025) without board approval. Began accepting ICE detainees November 2025. $102/day per detainee. Total capacity 286 beds.

AG Ellison's Dec 2025 opinion says unilateral sheriff signing is illegal. Agreements remain in effect. Community members demanding board discussion.
