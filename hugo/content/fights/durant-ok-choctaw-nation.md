## Overview

In one of the most creative and effective resistance actions against the ICE warehouse expansion, the **Choctaw Nation** purchased a warehouse in Durant, Oklahoma that DHS had targeted for ICE detention conversion — directly subverting the federal government's planned acquisition.

## Blue Owl Connection

The Durant warehouse was owned by **[Blue Owl Capital](/organizations/blue-owl-capital/)** through its real estate subsidiary. Blue Owl also sold the [Tremont, PA](/facilities/tremont-pa-mega-center/) warehouse to DHS for $119 million (double market value). The Durant and Tremont properties demonstrate Blue Owl's systematic involvement in the ICE warehouse pipeline.

## Significance

The Choctaw Nation's purchase represents a novel form of resistance: using tribal sovereignty and economic power to directly compete with the federal government in the real estate market. Unlike municipal resistance tools (water shutoffs, zoning challenges, lawsuits), a private purchase removes the property entirely from federal reach.
